Who We Are

Nayepankh Foundation is a registered non-governmental organization built on the belief that every individual deserves a fair chance at life. We work at the grassroots level to eradicate poverty and illiteracy.

By joining hands with thousands of volunteers across the nation, we are building a safer, healthier, and more educated society.

Our Key Features

Discover how we are creating an impact in multiple sectors.

Education

Nayepankh ensures that poverty does not strip children of their right to education. We provide free schooling and supplies.

Health

We organize health camps and distribute medical kits, focusing on preventative care and hygiene for rural areas.

Food Security

Through our hunger relief drives, our volunteers distribute highly nutritious meals to homeless and marginalized families.
